Lowongan PT Walden Global Service

PT walden Global Service adalah perusahaan yang bergerak di bidang IT yang berlokasi di Bandung dan Jakarta Raya. Gaji : IDR 3.500.000 – 7.000.000
Deskripsi Pekerjaan :

  • You will be involved in a dynamic team of web application developer and give the best support to the development team, you’ll also be in charge of providing high-quality codes.
Persyaratan (General Requirements)
  • Fast learner, detail oriented and must be able to work under pressure without loosing creativity.
  • Positive working attitude, willing to initiate, discipline again without loosing your creativity.
  • Well Communicate and self managed.
  • Fresh Graduated are welcome.
Persyaratan (Technical Requirements)
  • Strong Knowledge on OOP.
  • Strong Knowledge on web programming concept (client-server, HTTP methods, session, cookie).
  • Good Knowledge on HTML & CSS.
  • Working experience with Web MVC framework (Ruby on Rails/Cake PHP/PHP Code Igniter, Laravel, Symfony, etc) is a plus.
  • Have a freelance experience is a plus.
  • Familiar with Linux is a plus.
  • Familiar with subversion/git is a plus.
